Privacy Policy

CV Optimiser is designed to help you improve your CV and job applications. This page explains what data is used when you use the product.

What we collect

We may process the email address you use to sign in, your CV content, job descriptions you submit, billing-related subscription details, and the analysis results generated for your account.

How we use your data

We use your information to authenticate your account, run CV analysis requests, store your recent history, support billing and subscription management, respond to support requests, and improve product reliability.

AI-generated output

Your CV content and job descriptions may be sent to third-party model providers to generate analysis results. You should review all generated content carefully before using it in any application.

Payments

Payments and subscription management are handled through Stripe. We do not store your full payment card details.

Support requests

If you contact support, the details you submit are used only to help resolve your issue and maintain service quality.

Retention

We keep account, subscription, and analysis data for as long as needed to operate the product, comply with legal obligations, and provide support.

Contact

If you have a privacy question, please use the support option in the product.
